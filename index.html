Project Deep Dive

Social Work Simulation: Streamlining UX

The Challenge

The original Social Work course utilized a complex file structure in the LMS with deep nested folders, which significantly increased support ticket volume and student frustration with wayfinding.

The Solution

My solution focused on **structural UX design** and **component development**. I utilized Articulate Storyline to design interactive, linear components, managed the embedding and data flow into Canvas, and created clear, external support guides (job aids) for both faculty and students. This centralized access and simplified the learner journey, dramatically reducing navigation confusion.

Secondary Project Deep Dive

MATH 112 Redesign & Attrition Reversal

The Context

A foundational math course suffering from historically high attrition rates which signaled a clear disconnect between curriculum structure and student success.

The Impact

  • Double-digit increase in pass rates.
  • Reduced student course withdrawals.
  • Improved course alignment and usability.

Action: Data-Driven Redesign

I performed a comprehensive course audit using LMS analytics to identify root causes, which pointed to misalignment and confusing LMS navigation. The intervention involved implementing a weekly modular structure with clear visual hierarchy, significantly reducing the cognitive load from navigation. Assessments were thoroughly restructured to ensure constructive alignment and direct support of measurable learning objectives.

Outcome Summary

The redesign resulted in a marked reduction in course withdrawals and a significant year-over-year increase in overall student pass rates.
