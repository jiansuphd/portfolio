Case Study #1

Social Work Simulation

Visual Transformation

Reimagining the Student Learning Experience

The legacy Social Work course was hindered by fragmented navigation and a lack of visual hierarchy, leading to cognitive overload and high technical support needs.

My solution introduced a centralized simulation hub. This "digital lobby" provided immediate context, streamlined access to Storyline components, and reduced support tickets by over 40%.

Case Study #2

MATH 112 Redesign

The Challenge

Historically high attrition rates signaled a critical disconnect between learner effort and outcomes.

The Solution

  • Structural Alignment
  • Cognitive Load Reduction
  • Measurable Retention

Strategy: Data-Driven Transformation

We identified navigation confusion as a primary barrier. By restructuring the course into clear, consistent weekly modules and aligning assessments directly with behavioral objectives, we eliminated "hidden effort."

Impact Measured

"Double-digit increases in pass rates and a significant reduction in course withdrawals within a single academic year."
