Project Deep Dive

Social Work Simulation

The Challenge

The original Social Work course suffered from deep nested folders, leading to high support ticket volume and student frustration with wayfinding.

The Solution

My solution focused on **structural UX design** and **component development**. I designed the storyline components, managed the embedding and data flow into Canvas, and created clear, external support guides (job aids) to minimize technical barriers. This centralized access and simplified the learner journey.

Secondary Project Deep Dive

MATH 112 Redesign

The Challenge

Historically high attrition rates signaled a disconnect between student effort and course outcomes.

The Fix

  • Structural Redesign
  • Constructive Alignment
  • Improved Retention

Action: Data-Driven Redesign

Audited the course to find the root cause (misalignment and confusing LMS navigation). Implemented a weekly modular structure with clear visual hierarchy, eliminating cognitive load from navigation. Restructured assessments to directly support measurable learning objectives.

The Result

We saw a double-digit percentage increase in pass rates and a marked reduction in course withdrawals year-over-year.
