Simulation & User Flow

Outcome

The Challenge

Complex simulation requirements needed seamless integration into Canvas for students across multiple courses in the program.

The Fix

  • Storyline Component Design
  • Seamless Canvas Embedding
  • User Flow Optimization
  • Comprehensive Support Guides

Integration & Support Complexity

The Social Work program required a robust simulation environment. The challenge was not just designing the interactive components in Storyline, but ensuring they were seamlessly embedded within the Canvas LMS. Students from various courses needed reliable access, and the flow had to be intuitive to prevent technical barriers from impeding learning.

Action: End-to-End Strategy

Design & Integration: I helped design the interactive Storyline components and managed the technical embedding into Canvas, ensuring the simulation communicated correctly with the LMS gradebook and navigation.

Support Infrastructure: I established the user flow and created comprehensive job aids, including student and instructor guides. I also led the troubleshooting efforts to resolve access issues for students entering from different course entry points.

The Result

Successfully deployed a stable, accessible simulation environment with clear documentation, minimizing technical support tickets and enhancing the student learning experience.
