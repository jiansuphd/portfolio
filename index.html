                        <div>

                            <h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">Diagnosis: Cognitive Overload</h3>

                            <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed">

                                An instructional audit revealed two root causes for the failure rate: confusing LMS navigation that forced students to "hunt" for content, and assessments that didn't align with learning objectives. My objective was to transform the course into a learner-centered environment.

                            </p>

                        </div>


                        <!-- Action -->

                        <div>

                            <h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">Action: Structural & Pedagogical Redesign</h3>

                            <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">

                                <strong>Structural Redesign:</strong> I reorganized the content into consistent weekly modules and built a visual homepage to create a clear, linear learning path.

                            </p>

                            <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed">

                                <strong>Pedagogical Alignment:</strong> I restructured the assessment strategy to ensure every high-stakes exam was directly supported by measurable objectives and preparatory practice modules.

                            </p>

                        </div>

                            <p class="text-green-900 font-medium">

                                We saw a double-digit percentage increase in pass rates and a marked reduction in course withdrawals year-over-year.

                            </p>
